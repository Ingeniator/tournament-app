## Our Differentiators

### 1. Zero Friction
Most competitors require account creation, app download, or both. Tournament Manager: open a link and go. This is the single biggest advantage for casual organizers.

### 2. Works Offline
Courts often have poor connectivity. No other web-based tournament app works fully offline.

### 3. Format Breadth
6 formats in one app. Most competitors specialize in 1-2 formats or focus on traditional elimination brackets (which don't fit the Americano/Mexicano model).

### 4. Schedule Fairness Algorithm
Most apps generate random or sequential schedules. Our algorithm optimizes 5 fairness criteria simultaneously, producing provably better matchups.

### 5. Awards System
No competitor offers auto-generated statistical awards. This is a unique engagement and sharing driver.

### 6. Free & Open Source
No subscription, no freemium limits, no ads. This removes price as a barrier entirely.

## Positioning Statement

For casual tournament organizers who want fair matchups without the hassle, Tournament Manager is the free web app that generates optimized schedules in seconds. Unlike paid apps that require accounts and downloads, Tournament Manager runs instantly in any browser and works offline at the court.
