# Tournament Manager — Marketing Plan

## Product Summary

Tournament Manager is a free, open-source web app for running padel, pickleball, and racket sport tournaments at the court. It works offline, supports 6 formats (Americano, Mexicano, Mixicano, Team Americano, Team Mexicano, King of the Court), and runs entirely in the browser — no app install required.

**Live URL:** https://tm.padel.wiki/play

---

## Target Audience

### Primary
- **Club organizers** — coaches, club managers, and volunteers who run weekly or monthly social tournaments
- **Casual group organizers** — friend groups (8–20 people) who play together regularly and want fair matchups

### Secondary
- **Padel/pickleball communities** — Facebook groups, Reddit, Discord servers, WhatsApp groups
- **Sports facility owners** — courts, gyms, and recreation centers that host events
- **Corporate event planners** — team-building activities involving racket sports

### Geography
- Strong in padel markets: Spain, Sweden, Italy, Portugal, Latin America, Middle East
- Growing in pickleball markets: USA, Canada, UK, Australia
- App supports 8 languages: English, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Serbian, Swedish

## Key Value Propositions

| Message | For Whom | Why It Resonates |
|---------|----------|------------------|
| "Run a tournament in 2 minutes, no signup required" | Everyone | Zero friction — open the link and go |
| "Works offline at the court" | Organizers at outdoor venues | Courts often have poor WiFi/signal |
| "Fair matchups, automatically" | Repeat organizers | Schedule algorithm balances partners, opponents, rest |
| "Free forever, open source" | Budget-conscious clubs | No subscription, no paywall, no ads |
| "6 formats, one app" | Format-curious players | Try Mexicano or King of the Court without learning new tools |
| "Share results instantly" | Social players | Share standings as images or text to WhatsApp/Instagram |
| "Awards ceremony built in" | Fun-seekers | 26+ awards like "Giant Slayer", "Clutch Player" add excitement |

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Social Media (Organic)

**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X/Twitter, LinkedIn

**Content pillars:**
- **How-to demos** — 30-60s screen recordings showing tournament setup → play → results
- **Format explainers** — "What is Americano?" / "Mexicano vs Americano" educational content
- **Award highlights** — fun posts showcasing award cards ("Who's your Giant Slayer?")
- **User stories** — repost/feature clubs and groups using the app
- **Tips** — "5 tips for running a smooth padel tournament"

**Posting cadence:** 3–4x per week across platforms

**Hashtags:**
- Padel: #padel #padelamateur #padeltime #padeladdicts #padelcommunity #americanopadel
- Pickleball: #pickleball #pickleballlife #pickleballtournament
- General: #tournamentmanager #racketsports #sporttech

### 2. Community Seeding

**Reddit:**
- r/padel, r/Pickleball, r/tennis — helpful answers to "how do I organize a tournament?" posts
- Share as a tool recommendation, not a promotion

**Facebook Groups:**
- Padel community groups (country-specific)
- Pickleball community groups
- Local sports organizer groups

**Discord:**
- Padel/pickleball Discord servers
- Sports tech communities

**WhatsApp / Telegram:**
- Ask existing users to share the app link in their playing groups

### 3. Content Marketing (SEO)

**Blog posts / landing pages:**
- "How to Run an Americano Padel Tournament — Complete Guide"

**Target keywords:**
- "americano padel tournament app"

### 4. Partnerships

- **Local padel/pickleball clubs** — offer to demo the app at their next social event
- **Coaches and instructors** — they organize tournaments regularly, become ambassadors
- **Sport equipment brands** — co-branded tournaments ("X Brand Americano Night powered by Tournament Manager")
- **Court booking platforms** — integration or cross-promotion

### 5. Product Hunt / Hacker News

- Launch on Product Hunt with a clear tagline: "Run padel & pickleball tournaments from your phone — free, offline, no signup"
- Post on Hacker News Show HN as an open-source side project

### 6. App Store Presence (PWA)

- Submit to PWA directories and web app catalogs
- Optimize the web app manifest for "Add to Home Screen" discoverability

## Growth Tactics

### Viral Loops
1. **Share results** — every tournament generates shareable standings (image + text) that include the app URL
2. **Planner invite flow** — organizers share a join code; new players discover the app naturally
3. **"Powered by Tournament Manager"** footer on shared content

### Retention
1. **Award system** — 26+ awards create collectibility and conversation ("I got Undefeated!")
2. **Format variety** — once players try Americano, they explore Mexicano, King of the Court, etc.
3. **Skin customization** — personalization increases attachment

### Referral
- Add a "Share this app" option in the footer/settings
- Create a referral template: "I use this for our tournaments, try it: [link]"
