## Article 4: "King of the Court Padel: Rules & How to Play"

**Target keyword:** king of the court padel
**Secondary:** padel king of the court rules, king court padel format
**Word count:** 800–1,000

### Outline
1. What is King of the Court?
   - Court ladder system: winners move up, losers move down
   - Higher courts earn bonus points
   - Goal: reach and hold the top court
2. How it works
   - Courts ranked from lowest to highest
   - After each round, winning team moves up, losing team moves down
   - Points from match + court bonus = round total
3. How many players/courts
   - Works best with 8+ players on 2+ courts
4. Strategy tips
   - Early rounds matter less, but momentum matters
   - Adapt to your partner each round
