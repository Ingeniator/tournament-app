## LinkedIn Post

> I built a free, open-source web app for organizing padel and pickleball tournaments.
>
> The problem: running a fair tournament with rotating partners, balanced matchups, and proper rest distribution is a surprisingly complex scheduling problem. Most organizers use spreadsheets or whiteboards — and it takes 20-30 minutes of setup.
>
> Tournament Manager solves this in seconds:
> - 6 tournament formats (Americano, Mexicano, King of the Court, and more)
> - Algorithm-generated schedules that optimize fairness across 5 dimensions
> - Works offline — no WiFi needed at the courts
> - Live scoring and automatic standings
> - 26+ auto-generated awards based on match statistics
>
> Built with React, TypeScript, and Vite. Deployed as a PWA on Cloudflare Pages.
>
> No signup, no payment, no ads. Just open the link and run your tournament.
